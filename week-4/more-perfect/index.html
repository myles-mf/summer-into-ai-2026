WEEK 4 · BUILT ON YESTERDAY · the union now holds whatever you commit to it

Two weeks ago I made a million independent points hold one fixed shape — the flag. This is the same half-million sovereign agents, each following only its own pull, with no conductor — but now they'll hold anything you give them.

Type a word and commit it to the union — 1776, WE THE PEOPLE, your own name — and a half-million free parts assemble it out of nothing. Drag across them to scatter it into chaos, and with no one giving the order, they find their way back.

A union isn't the symbol. It's an equilibrium a half-million free parts return to — around whatever the people decide to hold together. (Runs entirely on your GPU.)
