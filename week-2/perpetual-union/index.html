PERPETUAL UNION

WEEK 2 · INDEPENDENCE ENGINES · independence is not a symbol, it's a mechanic

Every point of light you see is its own sovereign agent — half a million of them, each following only its own simple pull. No conductor. No central control. Yet together they hold one shape: the flag of the union.

Drag across them and they scatter into chaos — independence pulling every which way. Let go, and with no one giving the order, they find their way back into one. Out of many, one — and the one survives being torn apart.

A union isn't a thing that's held together from the top. It's an equilibrium a half-million free parts return to on their own. (Runs entirely on your GPU.)
